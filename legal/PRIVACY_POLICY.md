# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** December 24, 2024

This Privacy Policy describes how [APP_NAME] ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

## Information We Collect

### Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The App stores all your personal data locally on your device. This includes:
- Tasks, projects, and to-do items
- Habits and tracking history
- Calendar events and reminders
- Financial transactions and budgets
- Focus session records
- App preferences and settings

**This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it.**

### Analytics Data

We collect anonymous usage analytics to improve the App. This includes:
- App opens and session duration
- Feature usage (which screens you visit)
- Device type and operating system version
- Crash reports and error logs

This data is anonymized and cannot be used to identify you personally.

### Data We Do NOT Collect

- We do not collect your name, email, or contact information (unless you contact us for support)
- We do not collect location data
- We do not collect payment information
- We do not access your contacts, photos, or other device data
- We do not sell or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes

## How We Use Your Information

We use analytics data solely to:
- Identify and fix bugs and crashes
- Understand which features are most useful
- Improve app performance and user experience

## Third-Party Services

The App may use the following third-party services:

**Sentry** (Crash Reporting)
- Collects crash reports and error logs
- Privacy Policy: https://sentry.io/privacy/

**Analytics Provider**
- Collects anonymous usage statistics
- Data is aggregated and not personally identifiable

## Data Retention

- **Local Data:** Stored on your device until you delete it. Uninstalling the App removes all local data.
- **Analytics Data:** Retained for up to 12 months, then automatically deleted.
- **Crash Reports:** Retained for up to 90 days.

## Data Security

Your local data is stored using your device's secure storage mechanisms. We do not have access to your local data. Analytics data is transmitted using industry-standard encryption (HTTPS/TLS).

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access:** View all your data within the App
- **Export:** Export your data in CSV or JSON format via Settings > Data Management
- **Delete:** Delete any or all of your data via Settings > Data Management
- **Opt-Out:** Disable analytics in the App settings (if available)

## Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

## International Users

If you are accessing the App from outside the United States, please be aware that analytics data may be transferred to and processed in the United States.
