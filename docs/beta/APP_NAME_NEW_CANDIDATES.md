## Detailed Findings

### CRITICAL RISK - Avoid These

#### **Savi**
- **Major Conflict:** Savi Technology - established company (founded 1989)
  - Owned by Lockheed Martin, then spun off in 2012
  - Leader in IoT and supply chain visibility
  - World's largest RFID network in 52 countries
  - Multiple registered trademarks in technology/software classes
- **Additional Conflicts:**
  - Savi.ai - personal finance tracker app (premium subscription model)
  - Savi Finance - financial coach app
  - SAVI Home - smart home platform (rebranded from OliverIQ in 2025)
  - SAVi Health Technologies - registered trademark for AI health apps
- **Verdict:** DO NOT USE - too many established technology companies and active apps

#### **Mavi**
- **Major Conflict:** Mavi Jeans - major fashion/retail brand
  - Owns mavi.com
  - Active iOS/Android shopping app (34K reviews on Android)
  - Established brand with international presence
- **Additional Conflicts:**
  - Mavi.io - commerce API platform ($1 trillion in-car commerce market)
  - Mavi App - education platform for women (6.0 version released Oct 2025)
  - Mavi Area - fitness club app
  - Multiple apps by developer "MAVI" on Google Play
- **Verdict:** DO NOT USE - major brand conflict, active apps, domain taken

#### **Vida**
- **Major Conflict:** Vida Health - established health platform company
  - Founded 2014, based in San Francisco
  - Trusted by Fortune 500 employers and national health plans
  - Registered trademark for "V" covering extensive health services
  - vida.com domain taken
  - Active iOS/Android app with substantial user base
- **Additional Conflicts:**
  - My VIDA - electric vehicle companion app
  - vida.io - domain taken for different service
- **Verdict:** DO NOT USE - major company with registered trademarks, domain taken
