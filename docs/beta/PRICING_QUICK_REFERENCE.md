## Recommended Pricing

### Free Tier: "Jarvis Lite"
- Core features (tasks, 3 habits, 3 finance accounts, calendar view, focus timer)
- **15 AI messages per day**
- Cost to serve: $0.055/user/month

### Pro Tier: "Jarvis Pro"
- **$9.99/month** or **$83.88/year** (30% annual discount)
- Unlimited everything + 200 AI messages/day
- Advanced features (AI vision/voice, analytics, themes)
- Cost to serve: $0.168/user/month

## Decision Framework

**Why $9.99/month:**
- Under $10 psychological threshold
- 2x median standalone app ($4-5/month)
- 50% of ChatGPT Plus ($20/month)
- Same as TickTick + Habitify combined

**Why 15 free AI messages/day:**
- Covers light usage (3-5 msgs/day actual)
- Demonstrates value without giving too much
- Costs $0.053/user/month (affordable at scale)
- Encourages upgrade for power users (40+/day)

**Why freemium over paid-only:**
- Faster growth (low barrier to entry)
- Viral potential (free users share)
- Industry standard for productivity apps
- Target 3-5% conversion (industry benchmark)
