## 5. Reddit Communities & Power Users (5 Communities)

**IMPORTANT:** These are for genuine engagement only. NO spamming. Build reputation first.

### Communities to Engage With

| Community | Members | Vibe | Engagement Strategy |
|-----------|---------|------|---------------------|
| **r/productivity** | 2.4M | General productivity tips, app discussions | Answer questions. Share genuinely useful tips. Only mention Javi when directly relevant and after building karma. |
| **r/getdisciplined** | 1.5M | Building habits, self-discipline, check-ins | Very supportive community. Focus on the "no zero days" mindset. Offer help first. |
| **r/Notion** | 500K+ | Notion users, productivity systems | Many look for simpler alternatives. Engage in discussions about what works. |
| **r/apps** | 200K+ | App recommendations, new app discoveries | Good for "what app do you use for X" threads. Be genuine. |
| **r/androidapps / r/iOSapps** | 500K+ combined | Platform-specific app recommendations | Direct app recommendation requests. Answer when relevant. |

### Engagement Guidelines

1. **Do NOT spam.** Reddit hates self-promotion.
2. Create account under personal name (not "JaviApp")
3. Build karma by helping others for 2-4 weeks first
4. Only mention Javi when someone asks for app recommendations AND it genuinely fits
5. Disclose affiliation: "Full disclosure: I work on Javi, but here's why it might help..."
6. Focus on value, not features

### Potential Power Users to Watch

Look for users who:
- Regularly answer questions helpfully
- Have high karma in productivity subs
- Review apps objectively
- Could become genuine beta testers/advocates

## Outreach Templates

### For YouTubers/Bloggers (Personalized)

```
Subject: [Their recent video/article] + Quick question about indie app coverage

Hi [Name],

Loved your [specific video/article about X]. Your point about [specific insight] really resonated.

I'm one of the makers behind Javi, a new productivity app focused on [key differentiator]. We're about to launch our beta and wondered if you'd be interested in early access.

No pressure for coverage - we just think you'd genuinely find it useful based on [reason related to their content].

Happy to send TestFlight/APK if you're curious.

[Your name]
```

### For Twitter/Indie Hackers

```
Hey [Name] - been following your journey with [their project].

Just launched Javi into beta - it's a [brief description]. Would love your honest take if you have 5 mins to try it.

No strings attached, just looking for real feedback from makers who get it.

[link]
```

### For Podcasts

```
Subject: Indie app story for [Podcast Name]

Hi [Host name],

I'm [Name], maker of Javi - a productivity app we bootstrapped to [milestone].

Would love to share our story on [Podcast Name]:
- How we [interesting angle]
- Lessons from [specific challenge]
- What we learned about [topic relevant to their audience]

Happy to share more details if it sounds interesting.

Thanks,
[Name]
```
